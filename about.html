About Us

ABOUT TAFSAL

TAFS for A Level Studies has persistently worked towards improving standard of education. We impart 21st Century skills by developing students to meet the challenges of the knowledge driven and extensively interconnected post modern world. Our balanced approach to disseminating CAIE education, fosters all round development of students by affording them opportunities to enhance their intellectual and physical prowess. We work incessantly to effect learning in the context of our social norms. No wonder then that our students clinch admissions in top universities both in Pakistan and abroad.

PRINCIPAL'S MESSAGE


With a reputation for outstanding educational achievement, excellent facilities and enriching extra-curricular activities, TAFSAL focuses on individual care, and inspires students to make the most of their unique talents. Located in the vibrant area of Karachi TAFSAL is recognised as a top A-level provider. We pride on the fact that the students form strong bonds with each other whilst being encouraged to participate in a wide variety of extra-curricular activities in a friendly environment. Students are able to benefit from experienced teachers, many of whom have CAIE experience of decades. Since inception, TAFSAL has enjoyed exceptional success in placing its students on high-demand courses of their choice such as medicine, engineering, commerce and computer science. Our students have also had considerable success in gaining places to study other high-demand courses abroad at Scholarships. Tafsal offers a strong system of student support, with a designated member of staff to support students throughout their time at the school. We have such excellent premises, that we have been selected as one of the few A Level schools to provide practical exams facilities to CAIE.

Every year, we have an advanced objective to encourage students to keep their steps with time, and we are confident that at TAFSAL, students in particular and parents in general will find an environment conducive of holistic development towards becoming responsible people of this post modern world.

We welcome you, students and parents, to visit us and meet us for all queries pertaining to A levels and to TAFSAL
